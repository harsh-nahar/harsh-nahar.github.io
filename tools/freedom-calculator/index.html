How is this calculated?

1. Financial Runway

Runway = Total Liquid Savings / Monthly Expenses.
This number represents your ability to survive income loss without changing your lifestyle. A runway of 6-12 months provides significant career negotiation power.

2. The Freedom Number (FIRE)

Based on the 30x Rule. If you have accumulated 30 times your annual expenses, standard financial models suggest you can retire early, assuming your portfolio grows at 3-4% above inflation annually.

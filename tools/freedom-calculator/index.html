What do these numbers mean?

1. The Runway (Survival)

This is your "Fuck You Money." It tells you exactly how long you can go without a paycheck. Having 6-12 months of runway gives you the confidence to quit a toxic job, negotiate a better salary, or take a career break.

2. The Freedom Number (FIRE)

Based on the 30x Rule (Standard for India). If your annual expenses are ₹6 Lakhs, you need roughly ₹1.8 Crores invested to live off the interest forever, assuming your investments beat inflation by about 3-4%.

How is this calculated?

1. Your "Real" Hourly Rate

We calculate your effective hourly wage by dividing your monthly In-Hand salary by your total monthly work hours (Weekly Hours × 4.33 weeks).

2. The Opportunity Cost

Money spent today is money that cannot grow. We use the standard Compound Interest Formula: P × (1 + r/100)^t.
By default, we assume a 12% return (Index Funds) over 10 years, but you can adjust these in the settings.

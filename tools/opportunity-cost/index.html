Why calculate this?

Price tags are misleading. They show currency, but you pay with time. When you realize a new phone costs 25 days of sitting in an office, the urge to buy often disappears.

Furthermore, every Rupee you spend is a Rupee that cannot work for you. The "Future Value" shows the Opportunity Cost—the wealth you destroy by spending that money today instead of investing it.

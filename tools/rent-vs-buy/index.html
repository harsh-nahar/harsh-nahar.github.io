Understanding the Math

Scenario A: Buying

You pay a huge downpayment upfront. You pay monthly EMIs to the bank. You also pay maintenance.
Net Worth = (Current Home Value) - (Outstanding Loan).

Scenario B: Renting

You invest your Downpayment in mutual funds. Every month, the money you save (EMI - Rent) is also added to this investment (SIP).
Net Worth = Total Investment Portfolio Value.

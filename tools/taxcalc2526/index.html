About the Calculator

This tool helps you estimate your income tax liability for the Financial Year 2025-26. It automatically considers the updated tax slabs and enhanced rebate limits to give you a precise breakdown.

How it works

  • Standard Deduction: A flat deduction of ₹75,000 is automatically applied for salaried individuals in the New Regime (₹50,000 in Old Regime).
  • NPS Benefit: Employer contributions to NPS (National Pension System) under Section 80CCD(2) are deducted from your gross income.
  • Tax Rebate:
    • New Regime: Full tax rebate if taxable income is up to ₹12,00,000.
    • Old Regime: Full tax rebate if taxable income is up to ₹5,00,000.

Important Notes

  • Education Cess of 4% is included in the final tax calculation.
  • Surcharge is applied for incomes exceeding ₹50 Lakhs.
  • This calculator is designed primarily for salaried individuals.

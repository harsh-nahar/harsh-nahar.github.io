Understanding the Math

This calculator handles the latest updates from the Finance Bill 2025, including the new slabs, standard deduction hike, and surcharge caps.

1. The ₹12 Lakh Safety Net

In the New Regime, income up to ₹12 Lakhs is tax-free via rebate. But what if you earn ₹12,00,100? Normally, you would lose the rebate and pay ~₹60,000 in tax. However, we apply Marginal Relief, ensuring you only pay the excess amount earned (₹100) as tax, not the full amount.

2. The Surcharge Cap

For high incomes (> ₹50 Lakhs), a Surcharge is levied.
New Regime: Surcharge is capped at 25% (even for income > ₹5 Cr).
Old Regime: Surcharge goes up to 37% for income > ₹5 Cr.

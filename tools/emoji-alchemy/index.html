How to Play Emoji Alchemy

Welcome, Alchemist! Your goal is to discover new elements by combining existing ones.

Gameplay:

  • Start with a few base elements in the "Elements" panel.
  • To Combine:
    • Drag an element from "Elements" to one of the two slots on the "Workbench".
    • Alternatively, tap an element (in "Elements" or your "Collection") to view its info, then tap an empty slot on the "Workbench" to place it.
  • Once two slots are filled, press the "Combine" button.
  • If successful, a new element is discovered and added to your "Elements" and "Collection"!

Controls:

  • ❓: Shows these instructions.
  • 🔊/🔇: Toggles sound effects.
  • 💡 (in result area): Provides a hint if you're stuck.
  • 🌙/☀️: Toggles between Light and Dark mode.
  • 🔄: Resets all your game progress (use with caution!).

Saving & Loading:

  • Your progress is automatically saved in your browser.
  • Use "Save Progress" to download a file of your discoveries (will prompt for name on first save).
  • Use "Load Progress" to upload a previously saved file and continue.

Happy experimenting!

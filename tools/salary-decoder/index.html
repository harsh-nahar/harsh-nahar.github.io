Why is my In-Hand lower than I expected?

It usually comes down to three things that companies include in the CTC but you don't see in the bank:

  • Employer PF: Companies often hide their share of the Provident Fund (12% of Basic) inside your CTC. So technically, you pay for your own benefits.
  • Gratuity: This is money set aside for when you leave the company after 5 years. If you leave early, you often lose this part of your CTC entirely.
  • Taxes (TDS): The In-Hand calculator above estimates your income tax based on the regime you selected. This is deducted every month before you get paid.

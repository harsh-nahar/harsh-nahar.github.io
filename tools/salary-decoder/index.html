Understanding the Math

The biggest shock for Indian employees is the difference between the Offer Letter (CTC) and the Bank Credit (In-Hand). Here is what is happening:

1. The "CTC" Trap

CTC stands for Cost to Company. It is not what you get; it is what the company spends on you. This includes office coffee, insurance premiums, and the government-mandated share they must put into your Pension (PF).

2. Provident Fund (PF)

PF is a double whammy. 12% is deducted from your salary (Employee Share), AND another 12% is often deducted from your CTC (Employer Share) before calculating your Gross Salary. It's your money, but locked until retirement.

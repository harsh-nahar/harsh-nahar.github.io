Understanding Your Salary

In India, the difference between what a company offers (CTC) and what hits your bank account can be significant. Here is a breakdown of why that happens:

1. Cost to Company (CTC)

This is the total expense the company incurs on you. It includes "invisible" components like the Employer's share of Provident Fund (PF) and Gratuity. You earn this money, but it goes into long-term savings, not your monthly account.

2. Gross vs Net

Gross Salary is what remains after CTC deductions. However, before this hits your bank, the government mandates deductions for your share of PF, Professional Tax, and Income Tax (TDS). The remaining amount is your Net In-Hand Salary.

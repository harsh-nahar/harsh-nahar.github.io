Why doesn't the math match?

If you are new to the Indian corporate world, the difference between your Offer Letter (CTC) and your Bank Credit can be shocking. Here is the invisible math:

1. The "CTC" Trap

CTC stands for Cost to Company. It includes money that the company puts into your Pension (Employer PF) and Gratuity Fund. You technically "earn" this money, but you can't touch it until you retire or leave.

2. The "Gross" Reality

Your monthly payslip shows "Gross Salary," which is CTC minus those invisible deductions. But you don't get Gross either! From Gross, the government takes Professional Tax, your share of PF, and Income Tax (TDS).
